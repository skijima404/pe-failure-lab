## Failure Mode Statement
The vision and strategy are not explicitly explained, so people join or participate without being given a clear account of what the effort is trying to do.

## Context
This tends to emerge when organizers assume the direction is already understood, or focus more on gathering participation than on ensuring strategic clarity.

## Mechanism
- People are invited into the effort without a clear explanation of the vision and strategy.
- Participation begins before shared understanding is built.
- As a result, the volunteer army contains people who are present but not strategically oriented.

## Countermove
- Explain the vision and strategy explicitly before treating participation as meaningful involvement.
- Check whether people can restate the direction in their own words.
- Treat unexplained participation as fragile participation.
