## Failure Mode Statement
The urgency case tries to recruit potential coalition members through buzzword appeal, trendiness, or conceptual excitement instead of making the stakes understandable and relatable to them.

## Context
This tends to emerge when advocates are energized by the idea itself and assume that others will be motivated by the same conceptual appeal.

## Mechanism
- The conversation leans on the attractiveness of the concept rather than on concrete stakes people can recognize.
- Advocates assume that enthusiasm for the idea will transfer naturally to others.
- Potential coalition members hear a fashionable initiative, but not a reason they should spend credibility, time, or effort on it.

## Countermove
- Remove trend-based explanations and restate the case using concrete burdens, risks, or missed opportunities.
- Ask what each target participant would lose if the issue remains unchanged.
- Validate the urgency case with prospective coalition members before using it to recruit support.
