## Failure Mode Statement
Platform as a Product design is not planned as a strategic phase, so the team moves toward delivery without creating product-level design clarity first.

## Context
This tends to emerge when the team understands Platform Engineering as something to build, but does not reserve a deliberate phase for product design, operating assumptions, and interface shaping.

## Mechanism
- The strategy moves from vision directly toward implementation or demand response.
- Product design work is assumed to happen implicitly during delivery rather than as a planned phase.
- As a result, execution begins without enough clarity about product boundaries, user needs, or design intent.

## Countermove
- Make product design an explicit strategic phase with protected time.
- Ask what platform-as-a-product decisions must be made before broad delivery begins.
- Do not treat delivery motion as a substitute for design work.
