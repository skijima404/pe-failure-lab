## Failure Mode Statement
The coalition is assembled mainly from the originating team or technical domain, and the member set is not expanded to cover the broader value stream affected by the change.

## Context
This tends to emerge when Platform Engineering work is treated as something the infrastructure or platform group can organize on its own, even though the intended change crosses team boundaries.

## Mechanism
- The originating team starts with its own members and does not intentionally extend coalition design beyond that boundary.
- Similar technical background is treated as sufficient for coalition formation.
- As a result, the coalition reflects the perspective of one domain while missing the wider cross-functional coverage needed for organizational change.

## Countermove
- Define which parts of the value stream need representation before finalizing coalition membership.
- Treat same-team composition as a risk signal, not as a default starting point to accept unchallenged.
- Ask which groups will be affected by the change but are not yet represented in the coalition.
