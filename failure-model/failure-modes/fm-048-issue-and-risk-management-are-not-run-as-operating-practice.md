## Failure Mode Statement
Issue and risk management are not run as an operating practice, so surfaced barriers do not move through a reliable path from identification to handling.

## Context
This tends to emerge when the team can talk about blockers, but does not maintain a durable practice for tracking, prioritizing, and resolving them.

## Mechanism
- Barriers are not consistently managed as issues or risks with visible state and follow-up.
- Resolution work depends on memory, urgency, or local initiative instead of an operating discipline.
- As a result, barriers are repeatedly rediscovered or deprioritized without real progress.

## Countermove
- Run issue and risk management as a real operating practice, not only as ad hoc conversation.
- Maintain visible state for blockers so they can be prioritized and reviewed over time.
- Treat unmanaged barrier lists as a resolution failure, not merely as a tooling gap.
