## Failure Mode Statement
The reasoning chain from current urgency to strategic vision is weak, so the vision is not clearly shown as the answer to the business pain or coalition motivation already identified.

## Context
This tends to emerge when urgency has been discussed separately for executives or coalition members, but the conversation does not carry that urgency forward into a coherent explanation of why this particular vision follows from it.

## Mechanism
- Business-facing urgency and coalition-facing urgency are articulated, but they are not connected into a clear strategic narrative.
- The group can describe pain, and it can describe vision, but it does not explain why this vision is the right response to that pain.
- As a result, the chain of thought feels incomplete, and the vision loses force as a grounded strategic answer.

## Countermove
- Ask what current pain, risk, or opportunity this vision is specifically intended to answer.
- Require the team to explain the chain from urgency to vision in one coherent narrative.
- Check whether both executive-facing and coalition-facing urgency can be traced into the same strategic direction.
