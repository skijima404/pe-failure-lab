## Failure Mode Statement
The platform team does not absorb the method into its own operating model, so Platform Engineering remains something the team performs episodically rather than something it consistently embodies.

## Context
This tends to emerge when the team can use Platform Engineering language or artifacts, but does not internalize the underlying product, adoption, enablement, and operating principles into its own default way of working.

## Mechanism
- The method is followed procedurally without becoming part of the team's internal decision habits and operating assumptions.
- Platform Engineering remains a layer on top of the work rather than a mode built into how the team thinks and acts.
- As a result, the team can produce platform outputs without becoming durably shaped by the practice itself.

## Countermove
- Check whether the team can explain and apply the method without relying on external scaffolding.
- Translate method concepts into the platform team's own recurring decisions, reviews, and operating norms.
- Treat method imitation without internalization as an institutionalization failure, not only as incomplete training.
