## Failure Mode Statement
Potential guiding coalition members push to start the initiative from the appeal of the concept itself, before clarifying the concrete issue it is meant to address.

## Context
This tends to emerge when people are already positively predisposed to Platform Engineering and can easily imagine its value in general terms, but do not anchor the effort in a concrete organizational problem.

## Mechanism
- The conversation centers on the attractiveness of the concept rather than on a concrete current issue.
- Participants assume that adopting a recognized industry concept is itself a sufficient reason to begin.
- Others are invited into the effort before a shared issue statement exists, so the rationale stays vague from the start.

## Countermove
- Require the group to name the current issue, loss, or pressure before discussing initiatives or structures.
- Ask each prospective coalition member to explain in their own words what problem this effort is responding to.
- Delay coalition-building discussion until the originating issue can be stated concretely and consistently.
