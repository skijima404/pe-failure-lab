## Failure Mode Statement
The practice remains vendor-driven and does not survive contract boundaries, so Platform Engineering behavior weakens as soon as the external driver is reduced or removed.

## Context
This tends to emerge when vendors, consultants, or external partners are the main carriers of the method, and the internal platform team does not become the durable owner of the practice.

## Mechanism
- External actors provide the energy, structure, and operating logic that should have been absorbed internally.
- The internal team depends on vendor presence to keep the method coherent and active.
- As a result, the practice risks fading when contracts end, scope narrows, or external attention shifts away.

## Countermove
- Deliberately transfer operating ownership of the method into the internal platform team before external support winds down.
- Ask what parts of the practice would stop if the vendor disappeared tomorrow.
- Treat vendor continuity as temporary support, not as the long-term container for the practice.
