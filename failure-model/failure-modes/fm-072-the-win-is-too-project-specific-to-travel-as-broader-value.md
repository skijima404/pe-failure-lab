## Failure Mode Statement
The win is too project-specific to travel as broader value, so other audiences do not recognize it as something that matters beyond the local case.

## Context
This tends to emerge when a result helps one project or one narrow context, but is not framed or shaped in a way that lets others see it as a reusable or organization-relevant success.

## Mechanism
- The delivered result is tightly bound to local conditions, language, or needs.
- Potential recipients cannot tell why the result should matter to them or how it connects to broader platform value.
- As a result, the win stays local instead of becoming shared organizational evidence.

## Countermove
- Frame project-level results in terms of what pattern, reusable leverage, or broader lesson they prove.
- Ask whether people outside the local case can recognize why the win matters.
- Treat project-specific success as incomplete until its broader relevance is made legible.
