## Failure Mode Statement
Organizational change work does not continue into the adoption phase, so the effort launches the platform but does not keep shaping how the organization should notice, interpret, and stabilize its use.

## Context
This tends to emerge when change work is treated as something needed to start the initiative, but not as something that must continue while adoption is being normalized and made durable.

## Mechanism
- OCM-like reinforcement, framing, and adoption-shaping activity drops away once the offering exists.
- The team assumes that delivery will naturally convert into durable organizational use without continued change work.
- As a result, adoption remains under-shaped and easier to reverse when attention shifts.

## Countermove
- Continue organizational change work through the adoption phase instead of ending it at launch.
- Treat interpretation, reinforcement, and normalization as part of making adoption durable.
- Ask what is actively shaping the organization toward continued use after delivery exists.
