## Failure Mode Statement
Win delivery is not made visible enough to be recognized, so relevant stakeholders do not even realize that a short-term win has landed.

## Context
This tends to emerge when the team assumes that delivered value will speak for itself, but does not make the arrival, meaning, and significance of the result visible to the people who should notice it.

## Mechanism
- Useful delivery occurs without enough announcement, explanation, or visibility around it.
- Stakeholders are left to discover the win indirectly, late, or not at all.
- As a result, a real win fails to register as shared evidence because too few people know it happened.

## Countermove
- Treat win visibility as part of delivery, not as optional follow-up communication.
- Make it explicit when a win has landed, for whom it matters, and what it proves.
- Check whether the intended audience can actually name the recent win without prompting.
