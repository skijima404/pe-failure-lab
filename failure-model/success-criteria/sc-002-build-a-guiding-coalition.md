## Description
Stakeholders with the influence, decision authority, and execution responsibility needed to advance Platform Engineering change are aligned and acting as a coalition rather than as an isolated team.

## Rationale
A platform team alone cannot fully shift cross-organizational priorities, standardization, or responsibility boundaries. If the change effort depends on too narrow a group, it shrinks into well-intentioned local improvement and becomes too weak to withstand resistance or indifference.

## Evaluation Perspective
- Are key stakeholders outside the platform team included in the change effort?
- Is the coalition visible in actual decisions and support behavior, not just in name?
- Are missing authority or influence gaps being addressed rather than ignored?

## Observable Indicators
- Leaders or key contributors from multiple functions are engaged on an ongoing basis
- Important decisions are being advanced through the coalition
- Messages and actions supporting the change are visible outside the platform team
