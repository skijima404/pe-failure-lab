## Description
People who support the change and voluntarily cooperate with, adopt, and advocate for it are spreading beyond the platform team, reducing dependence on a small core group.

## Rationale
Platform Engineering does not take root unless behavior changes on the user side as well. Passive usage leads only to shallow adoption, turning the effort into something the platform team pushes onto others. Without growing voluntary supporters, both sustainability and scale remain weak.

## Evaluation Perspective
- Are there voluntary supporters and collaborators outside the platform team?
- Is adoption or advocacy driven by understanding and belief rather than command?
- Has the change effort expanded beyond being a platform-team-only activity?

## Observable Indicators
- User teams are offering collaboration proposals or improvement feedback
- The value of the platform is being explained by people other than the platform team
- Informal advocates exist who help drive adoption
