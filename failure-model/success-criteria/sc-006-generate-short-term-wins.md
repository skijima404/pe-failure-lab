## Description
Platform-specific value is demonstrated through observable short-term results, allowing stakeholders to recognize both the legitimacy of the change and forward progress.

## Rationale
Platform value often becomes visible only with delay, and when it is buried inside large initiatives or complex changes it is easy for people to conclude that there are no real results. Without visible short-term wins, it becomes much harder to sustain both investment justification and stakeholder support.

## Evaluation Perspective
- Is platform-specific value being demonstrated in a short, observable time frame?
- Are the results recognized as platform contributions rather than lost among other factors?
- Are wins designed in a way that supports continued investment and expectation building?

## Observable Indicators
- Reusable outcomes or improvement effects are made visible early
- Platform value is shared through specific metrics or concrete examples
- Wins are leading to follow-up support or additional investment decisions
