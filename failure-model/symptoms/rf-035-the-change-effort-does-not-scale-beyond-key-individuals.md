## Symptom Statement
The change effort does not scale beyond key individuals, so progress depends too heavily on the knowledge, drive, or judgment of a small number of people.

## Consequence
Acceleration becomes fragile because the effort cannot reproduce itself reliably across people, teams, or contexts.

## Observable Signals
- A small set of individuals remains essential to keeping the effort coherent and moving
- Others cannot easily pick up, repeat, or extend the way the work is being done
- When asked how the effort would continue if key people stepped back, the answer is weak or concerning

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the effort is impressive but overly dependent on who happens to be carrying it.

## Structural Impact
- Scaling risk stays high because knowledge and judgment remain concentrated
- Expansion is limited by individual bandwidth and presence
- Continuity weakens whenever key contributors are unavailable
