## Symptom Statement
The broader set of voluntary supporters does not form, so the change effort remains concentrated in a small core group.

## Consequence
The initiative stays dependent on a limited number of committed people, which weakens scale, sustainability, and broader movement beyond the core team.

## Observable Signals
- Attempts to broaden participation attract little real follow-through
- Support remains concentrated in the same small group rather than spreading outward
- When asked who is actively joining the effort voluntarily, the answer remains narrow or repetitive

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to see the effort as something a few committed people are carrying rather than as a change that others are choosing to join.

## Structural Impact
- Dependence on the core team remains high
- Diffusion of support stalls
- The effort becomes more vulnerable to fatigue in the initial core
