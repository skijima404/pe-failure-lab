## Symptom Statement
Usage and adoption are not tracked reliably, so the organization cannot tell whether the change is actually taking hold or where it is failing to stick.

## Consequence
Institutionalization remains guesswork because leaders and change agents do not have dependable visibility into real usage, retention, or regression.

## Observable Signals
- The team cannot state confidently who is using what, how often, or with what persistence
- Adoption claims rely on anecdote, memory, or partial data
- When asked where usage is growing, stalling, or regressing, the answer is incomplete or uncertain

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the platform may be taking hold, but not in a way that can be clearly seen or managed.

## Structural Impact
- Regression risk stays hidden
- Institutional progress is harder to steer or defend
- The effort loses leverage because adoption remains weakly observable
