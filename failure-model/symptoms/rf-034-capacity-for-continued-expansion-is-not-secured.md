## Symptom Statement
Capacity for continued expansion is not secured, so the effort cannot keep growing even when there is willingness or evidence to do more.

## Consequence
Acceleration stalls because people, time, or supporting resources are too thin to carry the effort beyond its initial success.

## Observable Signals
- Follow-on work is desired, but no real capacity is allocated for it
- Contributors are too tired, overcommitted, or under-supported to keep expanding the effort
- When asked what resources support the next phase, the answer is vague, fragile, or wishful

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the effort should continue, but lacks the practical capacity to do so without strain.

## Structural Impact
- Expansion becomes dependent on overextension rather than design
- The effort becomes vulnerable to burnout and under-delivery
- Growth opportunities are lost because support does not scale with ambition
