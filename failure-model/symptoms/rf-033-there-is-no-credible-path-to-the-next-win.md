## Symptom Statement
There is no credible path to the next win, so the effort cannot connect current success to the next visible step in a believable way.

## Consequence
Momentum weakens because stakeholders cannot see what comes next, how long it will take, or why further acceleration should feel realistic.

## Observable Signals
- The next win is undefined, too distant, or not supported by a believable path
- The roadmap after the first win is missing, thin, or aspirational only
- When asked what the next concrete win is, the answer is uncertain or far away

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the effort may continue somehow, but not through a convincing sequence of next results.

## Structural Impact
- Short-term reinforcement does not chain into continued progress
- Future change becomes harder to plan and justify
- The effort becomes vulnerable to losing narrative continuity
