## Symptom Statement
Momentum falls after the initial win, so the effort loses energy and forward motion instead of turning early success into continued acceleration.

## Consequence
The change effort appears to succeed once, but then settles into stasis or drift because the initial energy is not converted into sustained movement.

## Observable Signals
- People talk about what was already achieved more than what should happen next
- Energy and urgency drop noticeably after the first visible success
- When asked what acceleration looks like from here, the answer is thin, vague, or absent

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the effort had a moment of success, but is no longer acting like a growing change movement.

## Structural Impact
- The effort risks slipping from acceleration into maintenance or stagnation
- Early trust is not converted into continued change
- Attention begins to decay after the first success event
