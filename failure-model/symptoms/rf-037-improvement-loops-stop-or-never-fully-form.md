## Symptom Statement
Improvement loops stop or never fully form, so the effort loses the recurring mechanisms needed to keep team operation and product evolution improving over time.

## Consequence
Acceleration fades because learning is no longer converted into repeated operating or product improvement.

## Observable Signals
- Team-operating improvements stop being reviewed, adjusted, or reinforced
- Product improvements stop flowing through a recurring feedback and change cycle
- When asked how the effort learns and improves from cycle to cycle, the answer is incomplete, outdated, or missing

## User Interpretation
Participants are likely to feel that the effort improved once or twice, but not as part of a durable self-renewing rhythm.

## Structural Impact
- Learning fails to accumulate into operating advantage
- Product evolution loses continuity
- The effort becomes less adaptive over time
